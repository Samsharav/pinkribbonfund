Join the Pink Ribbon Fund!

The Pink Ribbon Fund was established to raise public awareness of breast cancer, promote prevention, and provide tangible support throughout the journey to recovery. Our activities include organizing fundraising campaigns, conducting research, and hosting educational workshops and seminars. We are committed to providing psychological support and information for survivors, as well as assisting with the purchase of essential medication, specialized bras, and breast prostheses.
